Careers

OUR PEOPLE ARE OUR GREATEST ASSET.

Nucleous3 is committed to sourcing, recruiting, training and developing local professionals to meet our customer's technical manpower needs. We are focused on providing technical learning and updating professional competence of our staff in a performance driven environment. We encourage our professionals to be imaginative, innovative and resourceful in their approach to every operational challenge we help our clients overcome.

One of our key objectives is to deepen local capacity through a combination of on-the-job training, local and international training and certification. In addition,we work with our international partners for skill transfer and global best work practices cross learning.

We invests considerably in localization programs to ensure all local employees are provided with the tools necessary to develop their technical, managerial and leadership skills and deliver safely, efficiently and satisfactorily. We are a competitive employer and promote a balanced reward system that attracts, develop, motivate and retain employees who are committed to excellence, productivity and efficiency.

So, if you are a performer, believes in team work, diversity and is ready for an exciting challenging career. Nucleous3 will provide you the opportunity to learn, grow and have fun in an inspiring work environment where you are treated fairly, recognized and rewarded for performance.
