CSR

Nucleous3 corporate social responsibility (CSR) program are designed as an ongoing social commitment and duty. We have set objectives that is focused on responsibly impacting  our  society in a positive longer term  outlook in addition to our  responsibility to other stakeholders-  our employees, customers, shareholders and  partners. We:

Maintain a Socially responsibility organization

Nucleous3 has adopted a proactive front-end approach in our societal roles. We incorporate ethical decision making in all aspects of our business operations; thus ensuring the presence we establish within our communities encourages fair, holistic development and growth of our host environment, economy and social dynamics.

We believe in contributing to the achievement of the vision 2020 vision and the Millennium development goals and this informs the CSR partners and alliances we form within and outside Nigeria. Our specific focus is to target social and economic catalysts programme that would help people unleash themselves and their surroundings. We target the Less privileged and commit funds in areas of education, Health, Culture and Micro business development.

Respect the Environment The Oil and gas industry is an Environment sensitive industry. Wherever we work, Nucleous3 will conduct our activities  and business with an overriding sense of environmental  friendliness , respect and responsibility.
We will therefore take opportunities to participate in promoting programmes and businesses growing businesses that is sensitive to ecological conservation, carbon footprint and land takes , and where possible support renewable energy sources,  emissions control ,  environmental awareness training etc..
